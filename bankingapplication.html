Road Rash Junior

The Road Rash Junior game provides its user a racing experience. In this game the user can use WASD format to move the player car. As part of the game opponent cars come towards the player car and the user needs to adjust the position of the player car. The more opponent cars the player avoids, the more score we are awarded. The user is alo provided with option to choose the level of difficulty at any point during the game. Also, the difficulty of the game keeps increasing after every ten increments in our score. Overall, the Road Rash Junior Game provides entertainment to its user and it can be played by any person who is above 3 years of age.
